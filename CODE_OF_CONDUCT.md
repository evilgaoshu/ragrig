# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in RAGRig a respectful, harassment-free experience
for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity
and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, sexual identity and orientation, or technology choices.

## Expected Behavior

- Be kind, patient, and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still naming risks clearly.
- Give and receive feedback with respect.
- Keep discussions focused on improving the project and its community.
- Respect privacy, security boundaries, and sensitive operational details.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, threats, stalking, or sustained disruption.
- Discriminatory jokes, comments, imagery, or exclusionary language.
- Publishing private information without explicit permission.
- Sexualized attention or content in project spaces.
- Deliberately bypassing security, privacy, or moderation decisions.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing acceptable behavior.
They may remove comments, commits, issues, pull requests, or other contributions
that violate this Code of Conduct. Maintainers may also temporarily or permanently
restrict participation for behavior they determine to be inappropriate,
threatening, offensive, or harmful.

## Reporting

Report concerns privately through the project's security or maintainer contact
channels. If the issue involves a maintainer, contact another maintainer when
possible. Reports will be reviewed respectfully and handled with appropriate
confidentiality.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces, including issues, pull
requests, discussions, documentation, chats, events, and public representation of
the project.
